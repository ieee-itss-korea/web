## How to Join

To become a member of the IEEE Korea Council ITS Society Chapter, you need:

1. **IEEE Membership**: Join at [ieee.org/join](https://www.ieee.org/membership/join/index.html)
2. **IEEE ITS Society Membership**: Select ITS Society during or after IEEE registration

### Member Benefits

- Discounted registration for ITSS conferences and seminars
- Access to IEEE Transactions on Intelligent Transportation Systems (T-ITS) and other journals
- Distinguished Lecturer Program participation
- Global ITS professional network
- Local chapter events and networking opportunities

### Community Channels

You can follow our activities even before joining IEEE through:

- **LinkedIn Group** — Professional networking
- **IEEE Collabratec** — IEEE collaboration platform
- **KakaoTalk Open Chat** — Real-time communication
