The IEEE ITSS Korea Chapter was officially established on December 2, 2025.

As a technical chapter under the IEEE ITS Society, we support academic exchange and professional networking for researchers, engineers, and students in the field of Intelligent Transportation Systems across South Korea.

We look forward to contributing to advances in ITS through seminars, workshops, Distinguished Lecturer talks, and other activities.

Join our community via LinkedIn Group, IEEE Collabratec, or KakaoTalk Open Chat.
