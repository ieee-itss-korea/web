IEEE ITS Society is accepting nominations for the 2026 ITSS Awards.

**Deadline:** March 8, 2026 (Anywhere on Earth)

### Awards Open for Nomination

| Award | Recognizes |
|-------|------------|
| Outstanding ITS Research Award | Significant research contributions in ITS |
| Outstanding ITS Application Award | Successful real-world ITS deployments |
| ITS Institutional Lead Award | Leadership in promoting ITS technologies |
| ITSS Young Researcher/Engineer Award | Early-career contributions (age ≤ 40) |
| ITS Lifetime Achievement Award | Exemplary career contributions (age ≥ 65) |
| ITSS Best Dissertation Award | Dissertation completed within 18 months of deadline |
| ITSS Best Chapter Award | Outstanding chapter activities |

Self-nominations are not permitted. Three reference letters are required (one for Best Dissertation Award).

For details and online submission, visit the [IEEE ITSS Awards Nominations](https://ieee-itss.org/awards/nominations/) page.
