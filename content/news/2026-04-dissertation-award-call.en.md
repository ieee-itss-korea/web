The IEEE ITSS Korea Chapter is pleased to announce its **inaugural Best Dissertation Award**, recognizing outstanding **PhD and Master's** theses in Intelligent Transportation Systems, autonomous driving, and transportation engineering, conducted at Korean institutions. The award draws on precedents set by the IEEE ITSS Italy and Germany Chapters, adapted to the Korean context.

This award also establishes a pathway from local research excellence to international recognition: PhD winners with eligible degree dates will be considered for nomination to the Society-level [IEEE ITSS Best Dissertation Award](https://ieee-itss.org/awards/) at ITSC 2027.

**Submission deadline:** June 30, 2026 (Tuesday)

## Eligibility

- PhD or Master's dissertation in any area of Intelligent Transportation Systems, autonomous driving, or transportation.
- Degree received after **January 2025** — that is, the **library-registration date** is on or after 2025-01-01.
- Research conducted at a Korean institution, or in collaboration with Korean institutions.
- Dissertations may be written in any language; an English one-page abstract is required.
- Finalists must present their work in **live English** at the Chapter Award Ceremony — pre-recorded presentations are not allowed.

> **Reference date — important:** Eligibility is determined by the **official date the dissertation was registered at the university library**, not the defense date. ITSS Society applies the same 18-month rule based on this date.

## Topics

Submissions are welcome in any ITS-related area, including but not limited to:

- Autonomous vehicles and AI for ITS
- Connected vehicles, V2X communication
- Traffic flow modeling, control, and management
- Smart public transport, Mobility-as-a-Service
- Freight, logistics, rail operations
- Sensors, signal processing, computer vision for ITS
- Sustainable and smart mobility
- Demand modeling and travel behavior
- Privacy, security, and standards in ITS

## Selection Process

Two stages:

1. **Written review (blind):** Applicant identity is masked; reviewers evaluate the dissertation, publications, and recommendation letter.
2. **Live English presentation:** Finalists present in English (15–20 min + Q&A) to a jury with a majority of external/international reviewers. Live + camera ON; no pre-recorded videos.

## Selection Criteria

The four official IEEE ITSS award criteria, plus a Korea Chapter-specific point for English presentation. Each scored on a **1–10 scale**:

| # | Criterion |
|---|---|
| 1 | Technical Contributions |
| 2 | Significance for ITS |
| 3 | Quality of Publication Record |
| 4 | Quality of Recommendation Letters |
| 5 | Live English Presentation *(Korea Chapter addition, weighted as 1 point in total scoring)* |

## Submission Materials

A **single PDF** combining:

1. **Cover page** — applicant name, institution, contact info, IEEE membership number (if any), degree type (PhD/Master's), library-registration date.
2. **One-page English abstract** — problem, approach, application context, scope.
3. **Full dissertation** (any language).
4. **Publication list** — all publications related to the dissertation.
5. **Sample published paper(s)** from the dissertation — preferably in *IEEE Transactions on ITS*, *IEEE Transactions on Intelligent Vehicles*, *IEEE ITS Magazine*, or other high-impact ITS journals.
6. **Recommendation letter** — from the dissertation advisor or a PhD committee member.
7. **PhD committee evaluation report** *(optional).*

## How to Submit

Submission is via **Google Form** (single PDF upload). The form URL will be announced in April 2026 via the Chapter's communication channels (LinkedIn, KakaoTalk, IEEE Collabratec, website).

## Prizes

Three awards will be presented. Cash prize amounts and ITSC 2026 travel support are pending IEEE ITSS New Initiatives funding approval; details will be announced when finalized.

- **First Place** — certificate, plaque, cash prize, optional ITSC 2026 registration & travel support
- **Second Place** — certificate, cash prize, optional ITSC 2026 registration & travel support
- **Third Place** — certificate, cash prize, optional ITSC 2026 registration & travel support

All finalists receive a certificate of recognition.

## ITSC 2027 Society-Award Pipeline (PhD only)

The Society-level [IEEE ITSS Best Dissertation Award](https://ieee-itss.org/awards/) (ITSC 2027) applies its own 18-month window. The Society's expected submission deadline is early March 2027 — meaning the Society's eligibility window starts approximately **2025-09**.

PhD winners whose **library-registration date is on or after 2025-09** are expected to be eligible for nomination by the Korea Chapter to the Society-level award.
