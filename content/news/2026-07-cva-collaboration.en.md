The IEEE ITSS Korea Chapter is opening an international collaboration with the **IEEE ITSS Central Virginia (CVA) Chapter** in the United States. The CVA Chapter is based at the University of Virginia (UVA) and James Madison University (JMU), and is chaired by Dr. B. Brian Park (UVA).

As a first activity, the two chapters are discussing running their own 3MT (Three Minute Thesis) competitions and serving as international judges to cross-judge each other's video presentations — a valuable chance for our students' work to reach an overseas review panel.

We plan to broaden the collaboration step by step, including around international academic conferences, and will share updates through our chapter channels.
