## About the Chapter

The IEEE ITSS Korea Chapter is a technical chapter under the IEEE ITS Society, serving professionals in Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS) across South Korea.

### Establishment

- **Founded:** December 2, 2025
- **Council:** IEEE Korea Council
- **Parent Society:** [IEEE Intelligent Transportation Systems Society (ITSS)](https://ieee-itss.org)

### Mission

Our chapter is dedicated to:

1. Sharing the latest research trends and fostering academic exchange in ITS
2. Hosting Distinguished Lecturer talks and seminars
3. Supporting participation in and organization of ITS-related conferences and workshops
4. Providing networking and collaboration opportunities across academia and industry
5. Supporting students and early-career researchers

### About IEEE ITSS

The IEEE Intelligent Transportation Systems Society (ITSS) covers the theory, experiment, and applications of intelligent transportation systems.

Key publications:
- IEEE Transactions on Intelligent Transportation Systems (T-ITS)
- IEEE Transactions on Intelligent Vehicles (T-IV)
- IEEE Open Journal of Intelligent Transportation Systems (OJ-ITS)

Key conferences:
- IEEE International Conference on Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITSC)
- IEEE Intelligent Vehicles Symposium (IV)
