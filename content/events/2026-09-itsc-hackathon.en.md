The IEEE ITSC 2026 Conference Committee invites innovators, students, professionals, and ITS enthusiasts to participate in the **IEEE ITSS Hackathon** during ITSC 2026 in Naples, themed around the **"ITSS Smart Mobility Data Challenge."**

The competition is a 4-hour, data-centric event built on AWS Cloud services. Participants explore a heterogeneous "Data Hypercube" and propose solutions to real-world urban mobility challenges. No advanced programming skills are required — the focus is on data analysis and KPI optimization.

## Why This Hackathon Matters

- The exponential growth of mobility data demands novel management solutions.
- Participants gain hands-on, practical experience in 4 hours of building.
- Solutions are evaluated objectively — no jury bias.

## Example Challenges

The "ITSS Smart Mobility Data Challenge" focuses on problems such as:

- **Local Public Transport (LPT):** Network structure — routes, stops, scheduled bus and underground timetables.
- **Road Traffic:** Aggregated and anonymized GPS data from fleets (taxis, logistics) to map average speeds and congestion levels.
- **Micro-mobility:** Strategies for mitigating environmental impact.

## Scoring System

The competition is designed around objective optimization rather than juried evaluation:

- Participants submit solutions (e.g., a list of coordinates, a schedule matrix) — not code.
- Solutions are sent to an endpoint that runs a simulation.
- The simulator computes the KPI score and returns it.
- A **live leaderboard** updates rankings in real time.
- The winner is whoever achieves the best score in the shortest time (used as a tiebreaker).
