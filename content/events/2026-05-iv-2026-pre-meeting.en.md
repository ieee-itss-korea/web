**IEEE ITSS Korea Chapter Meeting #1 — Pre-IV 2026 Online Session**

### Talk 1 — Pwn2Own Automotive 2026

76 zero-day vulnerabilities, $1,047,000 awarded; regulatory implications under KMVMA enforcement (August 2025) and the 2027 milestone.

### Talk 2 — Teaching Autonomous Racing in the LLM Era

Live demonstration of GitHub Actions-based F1TENTH/RoboRacer Lab 2 (Automatic Emergency Braking) grading + AI-tutor feedback loop. The audience watches a buggy student safety-node implementation fail, the AI tutor explain why, and a corrected version pass — end-to-end in the cloud.

## Target Audience

- IEEE ITSS members and student members in Korea
- Korean researchers, faculty, and graduate students attending (or interested in) IEEE IV 2026
- Anyone working on autonomous driving, vehicle cybersecurity, or LLM-assisted robotics/AV development
