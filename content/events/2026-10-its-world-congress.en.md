The IEEE ITSS Korea Chapter shares news of the **ITS World Congress 2026**, coming to Korea this fall — the largest international event in the ITS field, held in Gangneung.

## Event Details

| | |
|---|---|
| **Event** | ITS World Congress 2026 |
| **Dates** | October 19–23, 2026 |
| **Location** | Gangneung, Korea (host city of the 2018 Winter Olympics) |
| **Theme** | "Beyond Mobility, Connected World" |
| **Organizers** | ERTICO – ITS Europe, ITS America, ITS Asia-Pacific |

With the World Congress hosted on home soil, we look forward to strong interest and participation from our members.

## More Information

- **Official website:** [2026itsworldcongress.org](https://2026itsworldcongress.org)

*This event is jointly organized by ERTICO – ITS Europe, ITS America, and ITS Asia-Pacific. The IEEE ITSS Korea Chapter shares it as an event hosted in Korea.*
