**2026 IEEE ITSS Korea Chapter Academic Presentation Competition (3MT)**

The IEEE ITSS Korea Chapter is holding its 2026 academic presentation competition in the **Three Minute Thesis (3MT)** format — this year's chapter academic award, simplified to enable broader participation. Participants present their research to a non-specialist audience in a video of **up to 3 minutes with a single slide, in English**, submitted as an unlisted YouTube link. Eligibility has been relaxed this year: the competition is open to both students and professionals at Korean institutions.

> **Registration deadline: July 22, 2026 (Wed), 23:59 KST.** Please register via the [registration form](https://forms.gle/GLNL9xL4kFHgJt6y8) before the deadline.
>
> *The ceremony time, the room, and the judging criteria will be announced on this page and via chapter channels once confirmed.*
